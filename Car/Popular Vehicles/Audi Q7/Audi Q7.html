Summary


Propelling the Audi Q7 facelift is a 3.0-litre, V6, TFSI petrol engine with a 48V mild-hybrid system that is capable of producing a maximum power
output of 335bhp and 500Nm of torque. This motor is paired to an eight-speed automatic transmission sending power to all four
wheels via the signature Quattro all-wheel-drive system.Inside, the 2022 Audi Q7 gets feature updates in
the form of a panoramic sunroof, virtual cockpit, four-zone climate control, cruise control, a large touchscreen infotainment system, touch controls
for the HVAC system, ambient lighting, electrically adjustable front seats, adaptive air suspension, and eight airbags.
