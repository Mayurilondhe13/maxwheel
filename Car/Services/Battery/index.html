How to Maintain Your Car's Battery ?



Step 1 .
Coat the battery terminals in terminal spray to prevent corrosion.
Step 2 .
Tighten the hold-down to prevent the battery from
rattling around.
Step 3 .
Wrap the battery in a battery heater to prevent damage from the cold.
Step 4 .
Check the battery fluid level and add distilled water if it’s low.
Step 5 .
Buy a battery that’s less than a month old when you replace yours.
