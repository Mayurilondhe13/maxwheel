Car Insurance


A car insurance or motor insurance protects you and your four-wheeler against any damages due to accidents, theft and third-party person or property. A comprehensive car insurance policy helps you save money when your car is damaged in an accident or due to a natural calamity. Car insurance policies are mainly of three types - Third-Party Car Insurance, Standalone Own-Damage (OD) Car Insurance, and Comprehensive Car Insurance. It also includes personal accident cover to the car’s owner-driver. Apart from being compulsory under the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, it is essential to have car insurance, and to renew it on time, because it helps you stay stress-free about your four-wheeler





Car Insurance Coverage


The coverage in your car insurance policy depends on the type of policy that you have selected.
Third-Party Car Insurance Plans cover the following types of financial liabilities that you might face in a mishap involving your car–



Physical Injury


You accidently injured a third person while driving your car? Don’t fret; we cover for the medical expenses

Death of an Individual


If an individual seems to die because of an accident involving your car, we cover for the financial loss.

Damage to the Property


Property damages suffered by a third party due to your car, are covered under this plan.

Accidents


Suffered an accident that damaged your car? Don’t worry; the damages would be covered under our car insurance plans.




Fire and Explosion


If your car catches fire or explodes, the damages suffered would be covered by us.

Theft


Why worry about the theft or loss of your car when we are here to secure you against it. Get compensated for the financial loss suffered if you lose your car to theft.

Natural/Man-made Calamities


Damages from natural disasters like cyclones and hurricanes or man-made perils like riots and strikes will be covered under our comprehensive plan

Personal Accident Cover


If you are injured in an accident involving your insured car, the cost of your medical treatments would be borne by us.
