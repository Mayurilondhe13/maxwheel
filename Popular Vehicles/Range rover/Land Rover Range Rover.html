Range Rover





“Attitude and style are written in the description of Range rover File”



The Range Rover is the most luxurious SUV built by Land Rover and is the flagship of their SVO line-up as well.
This is the mid-life MY2018 upgrade for the Range Rover which was launched back in 2015.
The MY 2018 Rangie takes cues from the newly-launched Range Rover Velar with the raked and sleeker grille, matrix LED headlamps,
new tail lamps and chunkier bumpers with the exhausts now integrated into them.
It is offered in both versions – the standard and the long wheelbase with the latter offering more than 100mm of additional knee-room for the rear passenger.
