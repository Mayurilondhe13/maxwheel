Land Rover Range Rover Velar





“ Rover that hovers on land that is one and only Range Rover. ”



Land Rover Range Rover Velar is indeed one of the finest and good looking SUVs in its category.
The SUV is available in R-Dynamic S variant in both petrol as well as diesel engine option.
The updated Velar is one of the safer, cleaner, and smarter SUVs in its segment and is also one of the most technologically
