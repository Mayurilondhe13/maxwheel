Land Rover Defender





“Be like Range rover never get struck ”



Land Rover unveiled the new generation Defender SUV at the 2019 Frankfurt Motor Show. The model is offered in two body styles that include 90 and 110 wheelbase formats. The model was later introduced in India in February 2020, with prices starting at Rs 69.99 lakh (ex-showroom). The new Land Rover Defender is currently priced between 73.98 lakh and Rs 1.08 crore (ex-showroom). The model is available in nine trims that include Standard, S, SE, HSE, X-Dynamic S, X-Dynamic SE, H-Dynamic HSE, First Edition and X. Powertrain options on the 2021 Land Rover Defender include a 2.0-litre petrol engine and a 3.0-litre petrol engine. The former is capable of producing 296bhp and 400Nm of torque, while the latter produces 394bhp and 550Nm of torque. Also on offer is a 3.0-litre diesel motor that is tuned to produce 296bhp and 670Nm of torque. An eight-speed automatic transmission sends power to all four wheels via the AWD system.
