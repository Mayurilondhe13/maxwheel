Honda Civic Price: Honda Civic price starts at ₹ 21.16 Lakh and goes upto ₹ 27.14 Lakh. The price of Petrol variant for Civic ranges between ₹ 21.16 Lakh - ₹ 25.40 Lakh and the price of Diesel variant for Civic ranges between ₹ 24.81 Lakh - ₹ 27.14 Lakh. Honda Civic Versions: Civic is available in 10 variants. Out of these 10 variants, 6 are Automatic (CVT) and 4 are Manual. Honda Civic Colours: Civic is offered in 5 colours: Radiant Red Metallic, Modern Steel Metallic, Lunar Silver Metallic, Golden Brown Metallic and Platinum White Pearl. However, some of these colours are available in specific versions. Honda Civic Competitors: Civic is competing against Honda City Hybrid eHEV, Hyundai Elantra, Honda All New City, Honda City, Jeep Compass, Tata Harrier, Toyota Camry, MG Hector and Hyundai Tucson.
